### Performance Tracking

Each topic should be tracked with:
- `topic_id` (the # number above)
- `niche` (1, 2, or 3)
- `views`, `likes`, `comments`, `shares`, `completion_rate` (from TikTok analytics)
- `published_at` (timestamp)
- A `score` = (views × 0.3) + (likes × 0.2) + (comments × 0.25) + (shares × 0.25)

Topics scoring in the top 20% should be duplicated with slight angle variations. Bottom 20% should be retired.
