## Niche 1: Psychology & Human Behavior Hacks

### Why This Niche Wins

- **Massive built-in audience.** Psychology content ("dark psychology," manipulation tactics, body language, persuasion) consistently drives 500K-5M+ views on faceless accounts.
- **Scroll-stopping hooks.** "5 signs someone is jealous of you" or "This body language trick makes people respect you" trigger immediate curiosity — the #1 driver of TikTok retention.
- **Evergreen content.** Human psychology doesn't change. A video from 2023 still performs in 2025.
- **High completion rates.** Lists (e.g., "7 signs...") compel viewers to watch through to the end.
- **Strong Creator Rewards eligibility.** Videos over 1 minute with high completion rates qualify for the program.

### AI Prompt Strategy for Script Generation

```
SYSTEM PROMPT:
You are a viral TikTok scriptwriter specializing in psychology and human behavior content.
Write scripts that are engaging, conversational, and optimized for the TikTok algorithm.

STYLE RULES:
- Open with a pattern-interrupt hook: "Most people don't realize..." or "Here's what nobody tells you about..."
- Use short, punchy sentences (max 15 words each, ideally 5-10).
- Keep each script 350-500 words total (targeting 75-90 seconds spoken).
- Structure content as numbered lists or ordered revelations (e.g., "Number 3 will surprise you").
- End with a call-to-action: "Follow for more psychology insights" or "Save this for later."
- Use rhetorical questions to boost engagement e.g., "Have you noticed this happening to you?"

CONTENT ANGLE EXAMPLES:
- "5 body language tricks that make you instantly more confident"
- "3 phrases high-status people never say (psychology explains why)"
- "The 'door-in-the-face' technique — a persuasion trick that works every time"
- "How childhood trauma shapes your adult attachment style (7 signs)"
- "Why narcissists always do this one thing — and how to spot it"
- "4 stoic lessons that will change how you handle criticism"

AVOID:
- Clickbait that doesn't deliver value (harms watch time)
- Overly academic language (hurts retention)
- Controversial medical/psychological diagnoses
```

## Niche 2: Wealth & Financial Literacy

### Why This Niche Wins

- **High CPM.** Finance content commands 3-5× higher ad rates than entertainment.
- **Low competition for faceless format.** Most finance creators show their face. Faceless accounts with strong visuals stand out.
- **Broad appeal.** From "how to save money" to "crypto basics" to "rich mindset" — there's content for everyone.
- **Authority perception.** Well-researched finance content builds subscriber trust quickly.
- **Shareability.** Money tips are among the most-shared content types on TikTok.
- **Creator Rewards friendly.** Long-form educational content >1 min performs well.

### AI Prompt Strategy for Script Generation

```
SYSTEM PROMPT:
You are a financial education scriptwriter for TikTok. Write scripts that make complex
finance topics accessible, engaging, and actionable for a general audience.

STYLE RULES:
- Hook with a relatable money pain point: "I wish someone told me this about credit cards..."
- Use analogies and metaphors to explain complex concepts (e.g., "Compound interest is like a snowball rolling downhill").
- Keep scripts 300-450 words (65-85 seconds spoken).
- Use the "contrast" structure: what most people do → what smart money people do.
- Cite specific, surprising numbers to boost credibility (e.g., "The average person loses $4,200/year to subscription services").
- End with a specific, actionable takeaway.
- Use "Here's the thing most people get wrong..." as a mid-video retention hook.

CONTENT ANGLE EXAMPLES:
- "The 50/30/20 rule — but here's the dangerous 1% that changes everything"
- "Why the rich use debt differently (and how you can too)"
- "5 money habits that kept me broke until age 30"
- "The 'latte factor' explained — how small daily expenses = $1M lost"
- "3 investing terms every beginner MUST know before buying stocks"
- "How I automated my savings so I never had to think about it"
- "Why a credit score of 750 isn't enough anymore (new rules)"

AVOID:
- Promising get-rich-quick schemes (harms trust and may violate TikTok ToS)
- Complex jargon without explanation
- Giving individual stock picks (liability risk)
- Anything that sounds like financial advice without a disclaimer
```

> **Hook:** "I wish someone taught me this before I turned 25 — because it cost me nearly $50,000."

> Here's the thing most people get wrong about compound interest. They think it's just about investing early. But there's a hidden factor that makes all the difference — and it's not what you think.

## Niche 3: Relationships & Social Dynamics

### Why This Niche Wins

- **Universal audience.** Everyone has relationships — romantic, platonic, familial, professional. The addressable audience is billions, not niche.
- **High CPM & advertiser-friendly.** Relationship content attracts lifestyle, wellness, dating app, and self-improvement advertisers who pay 2-3× more than true crime audiences.
- **Synergy with Psychology niche.** Both niches serve the "self-improvement" audience — attachment theory, communication skills, emotional intelligence — enabling cross-promotion between accounts.
- **Scroll-stopping hooks.** "The reason you keep attracting the same type..." and "If you've ever felt unseen in your relationship..." trigger immediate personal relevance.
- **High completion rates.** People watch relationship content to the end because they're personally invested — a key signal for the Creator Rewards Program.
- **Evergreen & endlessly scalable.** Relationship dynamics, dating trends, friendship psychology, family systems — there's no content cap and it never goes out of date.

### AI Prompt Strategy for Script Generation

```
SYSTEM PROMPT:
You are a relationship advice and social dynamics scriptwriter for TikTok. Write scripts
that are relatable, psychologically insightful, and emotionally resonant.

STYLE RULES:
- Open with a relatable hook: "If you've ever felt like you're giving more than you're getting..."
- Use a reflective storytelling structure: shared experience → insight → actionable takeaway.
- Keep scripts 300-450 words (65-90 seconds spoken).
- Use warm, conversational language — like a wise friend giving advice.
- Structure content as revelations or realizations: "Here's what nobody tells you about..."
- End with an empowering takeaway that makes the viewer feel hopeful.
- Use the "contrast" structure: what most people do → what emotionally intelligent people do.

CONTENT ANGLE EXAMPLES:
- "5 signs you're in a codependent relationship (and how to break free)"
- "Why you keep attracting the same toxic type — attachment theory explains"
- "The one conversation that can save any relationship — if you have the courage"
- "3 things emotionally unavailable people always do (and how to spot them early)"
- "How childhood trauma shapes your love life — 7 patterns to recognize"
- "The difference between healthy love and trauma bonding"
- "Why 'the spark' is a myth — and what actually predicts lasting love"

AVOID:
- Giving absolute relationship advice (every situation is unique)
- Demonizing any gender or group
- Promising "one weird trick" solutions (harms trust)
- Overly clinical/psychological jargon without explanation
- Making viewers feel hopeless about their situation
```

## Cross-Niche Recommendations for TikAuto

### 1. Content Pipeline Automation

```
For all niches, the AI generation pipeline should be:

[Topic Selection] → [Script Generation via LLM] → [Voiceover via TTS]
     → [Visual Asset Generation/Retrieval] → [Video Assembly] → [Auto-Post]
```

- **Topic Bank**: Maintain a pre-built bank of 50+ content angles per niche (rotating weekly).
- **Thumbnail/First Frame**: Generate the first frame as a bold text overlay — the single most important factor for click-through rate.
- **Hashtag Strategy**: Auto-generate 5 niche-specific hashtags + 5 trending general hashtags per video.
- **Posting Schedule**: 2-3 videos/day per account, spaced 4+ hours apart.

### 2. Recommended Tech Stack for Engineer

| Component | Recommended Tool | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| Script Generation | GPT-4o / Claude 3.5 Sonnet | Best quality for narrative/educational content |
| Voiceover | ElevenLabs API | Most natural TTS, supports multiple voices per niche |
| Visual Generation | Midjourney / DALL-E 3 | Custom images matching script scenes |
| Stock Footage | Pexels API | Free stock video for generic scenes |
| Video Assembly | FFmpeg + custom Python (MoviePy) | Frame-accurate video composition |
| Text Overlay | ImageMagick + FFmpeg drawtext | Lightweight, server-side rendering |
| Scheduling | TikTok API (via Business account) | Auto-posting |

### 3. Differentiation Strategy

To avoid TikAuto accounts looking "samey" (which TikTok's algorithm penalizes):

- **Unique Hook Templates**: Each account gets a unique opening format (e.g., one always starts with a quote, another starts with a question).
- **Voice Preservation**: Different ElevenLabs voice per niche (warm empathetic for relationships, warm female for psychology, energetic male for finance).
- **Visual Signature**: Each niche has unique color palette and font pairing (documented above).
- **Content Freshness**: LLM temperature set to 0.85-0.95 to ensure scripts aren't repeating phrasing patterns.

### 4. Monetization Scaling Roadmap

| Phase | Accounts | Videos/Month | Est. Monthly Revenue (TikTok Creator Rewards) |
|-------|----------|-------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Pilot (Month 1) | 1 per niche (3 total) | 60/niche (180 total) | $500-1,500 |

| Scale (Month 4+) | 5-7 per niche (15-21 total) | 300+/niche (900+ total) | $10,000-30,000+ |

> **Note:** Revenue is highly dependent on geography (US/UK audiences pay 10-20× more than developing markets). Accounts should be registered with VPN/proxy in target countries (US, UK, Canada, Australia).
